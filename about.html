Our Story

We are a mobile payment app that uses mobile money and bank cards. With the use of QR codes our customers are able to scan a participating store’s QR code, type in their unique pin on the app and that’s it- payment done! Our company was founded in 2019, after Tari, our founder, saw the growing trend of mobile money payments and identified a gap in the market.! We aim to bring digital services to those who are financially excluded. We are customer focused and want to harness technology to enhance and improve people’s lives. We want ordinary Africans to have access to ecommerce and digital services. Our aim is to open up a financial window to people who previously had no access to one. We are a young team who are dynamic, curious and want to make mobile payments accessible to everyone with a smartphone. Now we are getting ready to launch our app and want to bring you on the rest of the journey with us.Our motto is how can we help ? We are here to do just that- so please get in touch!
